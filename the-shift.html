Project Overview

The Shift explores the transition between the natural landscape and the built environment. The design moves from the organic greenery of the site into a structured, modular system — achieved by using a shipping container structure as a rigid framework, softened by a rhythmic facade of vertical wood louvers.

These louvers act as a filter, mimicking the rhythmic light of a tree canopy to shade the interior corridors. The building's form shifts to create open public plazas at the ground level, allowing the park to bleed into the architecture.

Across three levels, the project balances expansive community zones with private residential units separated by terrace separators. The building acts as a gathering space for people of all kinds of backgrounds.
