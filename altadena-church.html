Project Overview

A study of light as faith — shaping movement, space, and community through shadow and illumination.

This project explores the idea of light as a symbol of God and a source of hope. Light is used as a guiding element, leading visitors from the worship space into the surrounding communal areas and shaping each environment through the play of shadow and illumination.

The openness of the design creates spaces that are calm, welcoming, and connected, encouraging moments of reflection, interaction, and spiritual renewal.
